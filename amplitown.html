About us

Ampli teenage accelerator - professional training for teenagers aged 11-19 in the field of IT, design, entrepreneurship. Auditorium (Kyiv, Lviv) and online format all over the world! Each of our students receives a guide to the best faculties and universities for higher education. In this guide, you will find up-to-date information on the best IT, design and economic faculties, tuition fees, admission terms, financing, scores on international English exams IELTS, TOEFL; also links to application forms. In the names of higher education institutions, there are hyperlinks to official websites where you can find additional information.
